Who we are

The Company

Our history

Telling stories
that matter

Onyxay Lanzarote Producciones was born with a clear purpose: to tell the stories that brands and companies want to convey. We are an audiovisual production company based in the Canary Islands, specialising in the organisation and comprehensive management of audiovisual services.

We take care of all aspects of the project so the client can focus on what really matters. Our fundamental belief is that effective communication through visual media transforms brands.

Our philosophy

"The way we do it determines the results"
